Rod Wave

Rod Wave is an American rapper and singer-songwriter from St. Petersburg, Florida, known for his emotive style and powerful lyrics that often reflect his struggles with mental health and personal experiences. He gained fame with his hit singles such as "Heart on Ice" and "Rags2Riches," establishing himself as a significant voice in the hip-hop genre.
